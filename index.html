Voice Recording Guide

Optimal Environment

  • Silent Room: Choose a quiet space with minimal background noise
  • No Echo: Avoid empty rooms that create echoes
  • No Distractions: Turn off music, TV, and other audio sources
  • Stable Position: Sit comfortably and avoid movement during recording

Microphone Setup

  • Distance: Maintain 10-20cm distance from microphone
  • Position: Speak directly into the microphone
  • Consistency: Hold microphone steady throughout recording
  • Quality: Use a good quality external microphone for best results

Speaking Technique

  • Natural Speech: Speak normally without exaggeration
  • Consistent Volume: Maintain steady speaking volume
  • Complete Phrases: Use full sentences for better analysis
  • Duration: Speak for 15-30 seconds for optimal analysis

Best Practices

  • Record at the same time of day for consistent comparisons
  • Wait 30 seconds between recordings
  • Use the same microphone for all recordings
  • Record in similar environmental conditions
